Typographic Specimen Book

I made this book for a Typography class in which I explored the history of some of the "World's greatest typefaces". I designed, cut, and assembled the book.

'Get Out The Vote' Poster

As part of a campaign to encourage unlikely voters to vote, I designed this typographic poster inspired by Gail Anderson's stamp.

Magazine Spreads

These spreads were made for an assignment about genres of magazine design. I created sophisticated and young adult spreads to go along with this beautiful short story.

Professional Stationary

I had the opportunity to design professional stationary based on a wordmark I had made prior. Included in this project was a resume, coverletter, reference list, letterhead, business card, and envelope.
